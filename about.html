Our Story

Where passion for roasting exceptional coffee aligns harmoniously with artistic self-expression.

The Philosophy

Two Arts, One Shared Sanctuary

Artemis was born out of a simple observation: both a perfectly extracted shot of espresso and a fresh stroke of paint require absolute focus, deep patience, and intuitive craft. We wanted to strip away the clinical, fast-paced atmosphere of modern cafes and return to a slowed-down coffee ritual that directly feeds your imagination.

Our Environment

A Canvas For Every Guest

Whether you're an experienced oil painter or someone who hasn’t touched a paint brush since middle school, our relaxed guided sessions ensure you find your footing. Supported by natural golden hour sunlight, dynamic ambient acoustics, and rich aromas, inspiration naturally flows.
